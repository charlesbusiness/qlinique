Business Requirements Document (BRD) Clinical Facility Management System
1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Clinical Facility Management System will provide a centralized platform for managing patient records, treatment administration, antenatal care, clinical operations, staff activities, and treatment-related financial records.
The system will support individual patients, family accounts, corporate accounts, antenatal patients, clinical staff management, treatment monitoring, and financial tracking.
2. BUSINESS OBJECTIVES
• Digitize patient records
• Eliminate paper-based treatment charts
• Improve treatment compliance monitoring
• Track all clinical interactions
• Maintain complete treatment history
• Support family and corporate-sponsored patients • Support antenatal care management
• Improve financial accountability
• Generate management and clinical reports
